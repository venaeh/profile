Distordia's Guild Community

Distordia Guild Server is the home of the creative adventurers who make up the ArthLab Vtuber Group — a modern guild focused on education, art, and professional collaboration. This server is a gathering place for artists, creators, and students to hone their skills, share knowledge, open commission services, and promote their work in a world structured like an adventurer's guild.


  • As an Artist Platform
    → Distordia provides a space to exhibit work, discuss techniques, and build identity as a creator.
  • As a Class & Education Hub
    → this server provides classes, discussions and education.
  • As a Commission Market
    → Adventurers can open service stalls, find clients, and build a professional reputation.

With a fantasy guild & adventure feel, each member is an Adventurer of Art who develops together, not competing toxically. Distordia Guild Server was built as a community that is safe, active, and oriented towards skill growth and creative networks.

Arthirsty Community

Arthirsty Server is a creative community Discord server focused on the world of art and creativity, specifically illustration and visual art. This server provides a platform for artists and art enthusiasts to gather, share their work, learn together, and build relationships within a single creative ecosystem. Arthirsty functions as:

  • Community Hub
    → a space for discussion, interaction, and collaboration among members
  • Learning Space
    → educational media, sharing tips, and developing artistic skills
  • Commission & Promotion Space
    → supports the promotion of works and collaboration opportunities
  • Partnership-Oriented Server
    → open to collaboration with other servers or communities

Conceptually, Arthirsty is not just a regular chat server, but an organized art community that combines social, educational, and professional elements in one place.
